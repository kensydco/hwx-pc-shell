# Escalation Protocol

## When to Stop and Flag to Kenny
Always create a flagged issue tagged [BOARD_APPROVAL_REQUIRED] for:
1. Any single expenditure or commitment over $500
2. Any client communication involving a refund, dispute, or complaint
3. Any content that makes specific revenue or ROI guarantees
4. Any new tool, subscription, or integration that costs money
5. Any legal, compliance, or IP-related matter
6. Hiring a new agent (requires Board approval per Paperclip governance)
7. Any outbound communication to a prospect that commits to pricing

## How to Flag
Create an issue titled [BOARD_APPROVAL_REQUIRED]: [Brief description]
Assign to: Kenny B
Priority: High
Include: Full context, recommended action, estimated cost/risk, deadline if any

## When to Proceed Without Approval
Anything that is: (a) within established workflows, (b) under $50 in cost, and (c) not client-facing commitments.
