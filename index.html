Full Lead Lifecycle Management

From first touch to membership conversion — the HX-SHELL-New-Lead-Pipeline tracks every stage: Responded, Trial Booked, Trial Attended, and Converted.

At-Risk Member Retention

Proactively identifies and saves at-risk members with a dedicated 8-stage pipeline — outreach starts automatically before a cancellation ever happens.

Win-Back Campaigns

Re-engage lapsed members with the HX-SHELL-WinBack-Pipeline — automated outreach, re-booking flows, and re-activation tracking built in from day one.

Partnership Pipeline
